Why Borough Market works well

  • Friday hours: 10:00 AM – 5:00 PM (perfect timing for early dinner).
  • Location: 10–20 min flat walk from Tower Bridge along the river.
  • Senior-friendly: Ground level (no stairs), lots of seating, diverse food (bread, cheeses, seafood, pastries, hot meals).
  • If tired: Shorten the market visit or take a taxi directly there and back.
