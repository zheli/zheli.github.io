Tax/accounting notes

  • The company normally pays lease, insurance, maintenance, service, repairs, tire changes, and inspections for a company lease car.
  • Insurance and normal maintenance are generally included in the car benefit and are not taxed separately.
  • Nettolöneavdrag reduces the taxable car benefit krona-for-krona, up to the förmånsvärde.
  • When nettolöneavdrag fully offsets the förmånsvärde, the extra employer social fees on the car benefit become approximately zero.
  • Leasing VAT rule assumed here: 50% of VAT on lease is recoverable. Insurance is normally VAT-free.
  • This is a planning calculator, not final payroll/accounting advice. Ask your accountant to configure the payroll treatment correctly.
